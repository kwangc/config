## Form factors

- **Dual arm / bimanual:** two 6–7 DOF arms, grippers, workspace, payload
- **Compact gripper arm:** small bimanual (e.g. 3+3 DOF), wearable, tabletop
- **Mounting:** tabletop, mobile base, fixed — affects deployment & compute
